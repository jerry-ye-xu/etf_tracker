About ETF Tracker

Hi there!

Welcome to the ETF Tracker. I have recently been investing a small portion of my savings, and it so happened that it was right before the substantial dip at the end of 2018.

The market bounced right back, and I realised that if I had a system to keep an eye on the growth I could have optimised growth further.

Hence the idea of the ETF Tracker was born. It tracks the trend of price movement by comparing a long-term and short-term period of moving averages (SMA). Simple but effective.

Feel free to browse around and shoot me a message if you have any questions =).
